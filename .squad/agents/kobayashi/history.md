### 2026-03-18 — Publish-readiness SEO checks for llms.txt posts

- For new-post discoverability reviews on this blog, verify that any promised thumbnail or social-preview asset actually exists under `content/uploads/YYYY/MM/` before signing off on metadata quality.
- For llms.txt-related reviews, treat the current repo pattern as a flat `llms.txt` posts list. Public guidance at `llmstxt.org` also references `llms-ctx.txt` and `llms-ctx-full.txt`, so missing context artifacts are a spec-alignment opportunity, not automatic evidence that the current llms.txt output is wrong.
